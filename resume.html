About Me

After graduating from high school in San Antonio, I attended college at the University of Texas in Austin where I studied biology. Rather than continue on to medical school, I joined the Navy as an officer and was commissioned through Officer Candidate School in Newport, Rhode Island. I served in the Navy for 5 years and was stationed in: Sasebo, Japan; Charleston, South Carolina; Bremerton, Washington; and San Diego, California. After separating from the Navy, I earned my MBA in Interntional Business at Texas State University.

During my time at Texas State, I was exposed to a program called R-Project which we used to perform statistical analysis on data. The language for R-Project was very similar to programming languages like JavaScript or C++ and I found myself enjoying working with R-Project when other students were not. After graduating, I wanted to learn coding skills to make myself a better job candidate. I enjoy coding because you get the opportunity to create something and apply problem solving skills.
